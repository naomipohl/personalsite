Blog from my 2015 European Research Trip

A blog chronocling my summer 2015 trip to Groningen (Netherlands) and Palma de Mallorca (Spain) featuring some snazzy photography. This trip was half research and half study abroad. It was generously funded by Penn's University Scholars program. While in the Netherlands I studied urban planning and worked on a project regarding use of public spaces. In Spain I studied marine biology and planned a hypothetical marina, taking into consideration environmental, logistical and economic constraints. It was an unbelievable experience!
